Shree Aangan ke Baare Mein

Shree Aangan, Panvel mein situated ek aisa residential project hai jo aapko seamless connectivity aur smart investment dono deta hai. Sion-Panvel Expressway, Mumbai-Pune Expressway aur Panvel Railway Station ki wajah se daily commute bilkul effortless ho jaata hai. Upcoming Navi Mumbai International Airport bhi bas thodi hi door hai — travel aur bhi aasaan hone wala hai!

Shree Aangan mein sirf ghar nahi milta — ek complete lifestyle milti hai! Thoughtfully designed 1 BHK & 2 BHK homes modern families ke liye perfectly planned hain, with quality construction aur open green spaces. Schools, hospitals aur retail outlets bilkul nearby hain. Possession: December 2026.
