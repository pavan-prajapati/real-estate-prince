Ghar Khareedna — Step by Step

First-time buyers ke liye simple guide. No jargon, no confusion.

1

Budget Fix Karo

Loan eligibility check karo. Bank se pre-approval lelo. Hidden charges samjho — stamp duty, registration, GST.

2

RERA Check Karo

Har project ka RERA number zaroor verify karo at maharera.mahaonline.gov.in. Scam se bachne ka sabse acha tarika.

3

Site Visit Lo

Photos dekhke decision mat karo. Khud dekho — locality, construction quality, builder reputation. Main free pick & drop arrange karta hoon.

4

Agreement Sign Karo

Sale agreement, allotment letter sab ache se padho. Possession date, penalty clause — sab clear hona chahiye.

5

Ghar Pravesh 🪔

Keys milne ke baad registration, society membership, utility connections. Main har step pe saath hoon.
